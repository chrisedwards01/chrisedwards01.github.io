Diet Plan

Get Lean
  • Focus on lean protein sources such as chicken, turkey, and fish.
  • Incorporate plenty of vegetables into your meals.
  • Avoid processed foods and added sugars.
  • Choose whole grains like brown rice and quinoa.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  • Eat smaller, frequent meals throughout the day.
  • Limit high-calorie snacks and desserts.
Gain Weight
  • Increase your calorie intake by consuming more food overall.
  • Include calorie-dense foods like nuts, seeds, and avocados.
  • Opt for healthy fats like olive oil and nut butter.
  • Add protein-rich foods such as lean meats, eggs, and legumes.
  • Incorporate complex carbohydrates like sweet potatoes and whole-grain bread.
  • Include regular snacks between meals.
  • Consider adding protein shakes or weight-gainer supplements.
